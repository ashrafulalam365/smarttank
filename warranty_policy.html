1 Products Covered Under Warranty

This warranty applies exclusively to the SmartTank Device sold by Roboidy. The total warranty period is ten (10) years from the verified date of purchase, structured as follows:

🔁 Year 1 — Replacement Warranty

If the SmartTank Device develops any manufacturing defect within the first 12 months of purchase, you are entitled to a full unit replacement — either a brand-new or certified refurbished device — at no cost for the device itself.

🔧 Years 2–10 — Service Warranty

After the first year, your device is covered under our nine-year service warranty. During this period, Roboidy will provide free labour and servicing for any manufacturing-related faults. However, the cost of any replacement parts required during servicing will be borne by the customer.

✅ A combined 10-year warranty reflects our confidence in the SmartTank Device's build quality and long-term durability.

2 Products NOT Covered Under Warranty

The following items sold by Roboidy are not covered under any warranty and are sold on an "as-is" basis:

  • SmartTank Sensor
  • SmartTank Cables
  • Any other accessories, unless explicitly stated at the time of purchase
⚠️ These items are not eligible for replacement, repair, or refund under this Warranty Policy. Please check individual product listings for any applicable coverage.

3 Warranty Conditions & Claim Process

To qualify for a warranty claim on your SmartTank Device, all of the following conditions must be satisfied:

  • The claim is submitted within the active warranty period
  • A valid proof of purchase is provided (e.g., invoice, order confirmation, receipt)
  • The device has been used strictly in accordance with the official user manual and guidelines
  • The fault is clearly attributable to a manufacturing defect — not physical damage or misuse

How to Submit a Warranty Claim

  1. Contact our customer support team via WhatsApp, email, or phone with your proof of purchase and a clear description of the problem.
  2. Our technical team will guide you through a remote troubleshooting session to accurately diagnose the issue.
  3. If the defect is confirmed to be covered under warranty, we will provide instructions for returning or dispatching the device for service.
  4. For claims within the first year, a replacement unit (new or refurbished) will be issued at no charge.
  5. For service claims in years 2–10, Roboidy will carry out free servicing; however, any required parts will be charged to the customer.
  6. Shipping, packaging, and handling costs associated with sending the device for warranty service are the customer's responsibility.

4 Exclusions from Warranty Coverage

This warranty does not apply to any damage, malfunction, or defect caused by the following:

  • Accidents, physical impacts, misuse, negligence, or deliberate damage
  • Improper installation, maintenance, or operation outside of our stated guidelines
  • Unauthorized modifications, disassembly, or repairs by unapproved third parties
  • Connection to incorrect voltage supply or use of incompatible accessories or components
  • Damage resulting from natural events such as floods, lightning strikes, fire, or other acts of nature
  • General wear and tear, cosmetic deterioration, or surface corrosion over time
  • Use of the device outside the specified environmental and operational parameters
⚠️ Any evidence of tampering or unauthorized repair will automatically void the warranty on the affected device.

5 Support & Servicing

🆓 Free Remote Support

Roboidy offers complimentary remote troubleshooting and technical assistance via WhatsApp, Messenger, and phone calls. This support is available during official office hours and covers initial diagnosis, configuration help, and general usage guidance.

🏠 On-Site Service Visits

💬 We encourage all customers to start with remote support, as most issues can be resolved quickly without the need for a physical visit.

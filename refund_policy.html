Refund Policy

Last Updated: 07/02/2024

At Roboidy, customer satisfaction is at the heart of everything we do. We take great care in ensuring our products meet the highest quality standards before they reach you. This Refund Policy clearly outlines our position on refunds and what we offer instead to ensure you always receive the support you deserve.
🚫

We Do Not Offer Refunds

All sales of Roboidy products are considered final once the product has been delivered. We strongly encourage customers to review all product details, specifications, and policies carefully before completing a purchase.

1 No Refund Policy

Once a SmartTank product has been successfully delivered to the customer — whether collected directly from our office, received via courier, or handed over by one of our authorized support electricians — it is no longer eligible for a refund under any circumstances.

This policy applies to all products sold through our official channels, including our website and authorized resellers. Delivery is considered complete upon any of the following:

  • Personal collection from the Roboidy office
  • Confirmed receipt via a courier or delivery service
  • Handover by an authorized Roboidy technician or electrician
⚠️ We encourage all customers to thoroughly review product specifications, compatibility requirements, and our Warranty Policy before making a purchase decision.

2 What We Offer Instead

Although refunds are not available, Roboidy is fully committed to resolving any product-related issues through our comprehensive support and warranty framework. Here's what you can count on:

🔁

Unit Replacement

Manufacturing defects within the first year qualify for a full unit replacement.

🔧

Free Servicing

Covered repairs and servicing for up to 9 years after the replacement period.

💬

Remote Support

Free troubleshooting via WhatsApp, Messenger, or phone during office hours.

✅ All exchanges and repairs are governed by the terms outlined in our Warranty Policy. We strongly recommend reviewing it to understand your full entitlements.

3 Product Support & Warranty Claims

If you experience any issue with your SmartTank Device after delivery, please contact our support team without delay. Our team will work with you to identify the problem and determine the most appropriate course of action based on the nature of the issue and the product's current warranty status.

How to Reach Us for Support

  1. Contact our support team via WhatsApp, email, or phone and briefly describe the issue you are experiencing.
  2. Our technicians will conduct a remote diagnosis session to better understand the problem.
  3. Based on the findings, we will advise on the next steps — which may include troubleshooting guidance, a service visit, or initiating a warranty claim.
  4. If a warranty claim is applicable, we will guide you through the process as outlined in our Warranty Policy.
⚠️ Issues not covered under the Warranty Policy — such as physical damage, misuse, or unauthorized modifications — will not qualify for free exchange or repair services.

4 Before You Purchase

To ensure you make a well-informed buying decision, we recommend the following steps before placing your order:

  • Read the full product description, technical specifications, and compatibility information
  • Review our Warranty Policy to understand what is and isn't covered
  • Check our Terms & Conditions for a complete overview of your rights and responsibilities
  • Reach out to our support team with any pre-purchase questions — we're happy to help
💡 Our team is available before and after your purchase to ensure you have everything you need to get the most out of your SmartTank product.
